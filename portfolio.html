Week 8

At the Festival of Economics, Alessio Terzi claimed that CO2 emissions and economic growth intrinsically linked, both historically and in the modern-day.

The chart below plots CO2 emissions per capita against GDP per capita for different countries.

The chart below plots the percentage increase in CO2 emissions per capita and GDP per capita for the UK since their respective values in 1800.

The first chart supports the claim, since it shows countries with higher GDPs tend to have higher CO2 emissions.

The second chart refutes the claim, since CO2 emissions per capita have fallen significantly since 1970 in the UK, but the economy has continued to grow.
